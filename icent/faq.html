FAQ

How do I place an order?

  • Go to the SHOP section and browse through the selection of available products.
  • Add and manage desired items into your cart.
  • Double-check the items in your cart as we do not accept cancellation of orders once confirmed.
  • Proceed to checkout and fill out the form before your screen.
  • Modes of Payment: Debit Card / Credit Card or Online Bank
  • Wait for an email confirmation from our online sales rep, and your orders will be prepared and shipped soon.
